About Me

I am currently an academic master's student majoring in hematology in internal medicine at Xu Zhou Medical University. During this period,the research direction is the pathogenesis of leukemia.

My passion is to deeply understand the occurrence and development mechanism of leukemia, provide new ideas or new targets for disease treatment, improve the effect of existing treatment methods, and improve the resistance of existing chemotherapy drugs.

I am seeking a Ph.D position starting from Fall 2025.If you are interested in my research,please feel free to reach out.

Research Interests

  • Inhibitor on the biological behaviors of acute myeloid leukemia cells
